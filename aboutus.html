About Us

Barbara Thomas

My name is Barbara and I make something out of nothing! My major is Web Design with Graphic Specialization. I am getting a late start in obtaining my degree. My kids are all grown and doing thier own things will schooling and jobs. I finally have the time to do something for me.

I have been interested in building websites since the days of GeoCities, AngelFire, and the like. I loved typing what looked like gibberish and have it turn into an interactive web page with pictures and music! It was so gratifying when I got the code correctly written, I got a little rush every time.

Career Aspirations

As I am taking this class, it is all coming back to me. The excitement of making something from nothing! I hope to use my degree and find a job that I can do something that I enjoy every day. I would like to help businesses, charities, and non-profit organizations with their internet presence.

I believe that with the degree I earn from BrightPoint Community College will help me reach my goals.
